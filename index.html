What is Cookoo?

Cookoo keeps track of your pressure cooker so you don't have to. Keep it on your cooker and forget about it. Cookoo will tell you when to turn off the stove.

How does it work?

Cookoo monitors the temperature of your cooker. When the cooker whistles, the pressure inside it falls rapidly. This causes a sharp decrease in the temperate, which Cookoo detects. Cookoo alerts you when the cooker has whistled the number of times you had set.
